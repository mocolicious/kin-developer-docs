The Kin Rewards Engine rewards developers for creating awesome experiences that utilize Kin. In order to maintain a fair playing field amongst Kin developers and a safe experience for users, applications must satisfy these requirements to be eligible for rewards from the KRE, as well as our [Developer Terms](https://kin.org/kin-developer-terms/) and [Terms of Use](https://kin.org/terms-of-use-agreement/).

## User Payments

Transactions that involve a user spending Kin in an app must meet the following requirements:

<div className="KREChecklist">
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="accessible" name="accessible" value="accessible" />
  <label htmlFor="accessible">Users are aware of items/services they are spending Kin on.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="compliant" name="compliant" value="compliant" />
  <label htmlFor="compliant">Users are aware of the cost of each item/ service.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="terms" name="terms" value="terms" />
  <label htmlFor="terms">
    Users voluntarily initiate the spend transaction by performing an action such as clicking a button.
  </label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="accessible" name="accessible" value="accessible" />
  <label htmlFor="accessible">No transaction occurs without authorisation from the user to spend their Kin.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="compliant" name="compliant" value="compliant" />
  <label htmlFor="compliant">
    Transactions greater than 100 Kin have a confirmation prompt before the spend transaction occurs.
  </label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="terms" name="terms" value="terms" />
  <label htmlFor="terms">The Kin icon is displayed in the UI to the extent practicable.</label>
  <br />
</div>

## Subscriptions

The initial payment of a Subscription is treated the same as a Spend Transaction, however subsequent payments for the Subscription may occur automatically so therefore we have some special rules.

<div className="KREChecklist">
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="accessible" name="accessible" value="accessible" />
  <label htmlFor="accessible">
    Initial Subscription signup conforms to the <a href="/essentials/kre-checklist/#spend-checklist">Spend checklist</a>{' '}
    above.
  </label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="compliant" name="compliant" value="compliant" />
  <label htmlFor="compliant">Users are aware of what they are subscribing to.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="terms" name="terms" value="terms" />
  <label htmlFor="terms">Users are aware of the length and cost of the Subscription.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="accessible" name="accessible" value="accessible" />
  <label htmlFor="accessible">No Subscription occurs without authorization from the user.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="compliant" name="compliant" value="compliant" />
  <label htmlFor="compliant">The billing interval is not less than 1 week in length.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="terms" name="terms" value="terms" />
  <label htmlFor="terms">The cost of the Subscription does not change during the Subscription period.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="compliant" name="compliant" value="compliant" />
  <label htmlFor="compliant">The user has information on how to unsubscribe.</label>
  <br />
  <input className="KREChecklistCheckbox" type="checkbox" id="terms" name="terms" value="terms" />
  <label htmlFor="terms">The user can unsubscribe at any time.</label>
</div>
